Stephen M.Rice

CLU®, ChFC®, CAP TM

Steve Rice has more than 33 years of financial advisory experience. He is a Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU), a Chartered Fincancial Consultant (ChFC), and a Chartered Advisory in Philalthrophy (CAP). He majored in Finance at San Jose State University. A highly regarded professional in financial planning.Steve has been featured speaker at more than 200 meetings throughout the U.S., authored articles for numerous financial and consumer publications, and appeared in local television shows to discuss newsworthy financial topics. He is Past President of the Santa Clara County Estate Planning Council and Past president of the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisor-California. A lifelong Resident of Los Gatos, Steve is also greatly involved with the community. He was elected to the Los Gatos Town Council in 2008 and lead the communtiy as Mayor in 2011-2012. He has served as Chair of the Santa Clara Valley Water Commission, Treasurer of the Peninsula Division of the California League of Cities, Vice Chair of the Solid Waste Management Joint Powers Agency, and as Chair of the Santa Clara Country Emergency Preparedness Council. He is also the Past President of the Los Gatos Museum Association and of the Rotary Club of Los Gatos, and is a past Rotary Assitant District Governer. Steve lives in Los Gatos with his wife, Linda. They have two sons. Their oldest son Stephen lives in the Seattle area and works in the technology industry. Brain lives at home and attends Santa Clara University.
