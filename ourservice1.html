Our Services

Rice Financial Consulting is different — and that difference makes us better. Why? Because we view our clients and our profession differently than the typical financial advisor or insurance and investment broker. Our unique servicing approach has three main characteristics:

 
 
1

First, our clients’ lifetime of concerns are at the forefront of an unique offer, The Living Balance Sheet®. The result is that we have financially healthy clients with the financial balance they deserve. Our recommendations are always based solely on what is best for our client’s individual situation.

 
2

Second, many professionals in our business focus on their clients’ anticipated financial “needs” at some arbitrary point in the future and formulate their solutions based on these perceived “needs.” We recognize that there is no set timeline for when you will want to access and use your accumulated wealth. We are focused on building our clients’ wealth to the maximum level possible, while eliminating the potential pitfalls that are ignored in traditional planning.

 
3

Finally, Rice Financial Consulting routinely “de-bunks” the myths and false beliefs that have guided countless people into unsound financial decisions. Too many financial actions have been based on widely accepted misinformation, oftentimes perpetuated by widely read financial publications or espoused by “Financial Entertainers” on television, or in the mass media. Often, it’s a case of neglecting to account for one financial factor that could impact another. Or, the theoretical benefits of one investment vehicle will be selectively promoted, without looking at how other factors will affect its true yield.
